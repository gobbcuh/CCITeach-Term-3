GDG on Campus National University?

GDG on Campus at National University Manila is one of the student-led tech communities supported by Google Developers. It’s all about peer-to-peer learning, where students can explore Google technologies, grow their skills, and work on real-world tech projects.

They organize events like:

  • Workshops and study groups
  • Hackathons
  • Productivity sessions

It’s a great space for us to connect, learn, and build together.
